Who are we?

RMC is a car manufacturer founded by Italian Mr. Juan Rebuffo now run by its former grandson Mr. Angel Alba Rebuffo.

How did it all start?

In 1970 Mr. Rebuffo, a car mechanic, was asked to build a car by one of its costumers. This car is the RMC Corvega (seen on image being transported).

What about the present?

Nowadays RMC is marketed as a premium car brand with innovation and safety always as our goal. We are now based in Germany but have several factories all around the world
